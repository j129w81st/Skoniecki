Meal Details

Chicken Tacos with Bell Peppers and Onions

prep tips:

  • Slice chicken evenly for faster cooking.
  • Sauté peppers and onions in a skillet with a little oil and taco seasoning.
  • Warm tortillas in the microwave or on a hot griddle.

get ahead:

  • Dice an extra bell pepper for Wednesday’s stir-fry.

quick substitutions:

  • Use ground beef, tofu crumbles, or refried beans for varied protein.

extra thoughts:

  • Serve tacos with fresh salsa or guacamole for added flavor.

order of operations:

  • Season and cook chicken thoroughly.
  • Sauté peppers and onions.
  • Warm tortillas.
  • Assemble tacos and serve.
