Beef Stir-Fry with Broccoli and Snap Peas

prep tips:

  • Slice beef thinly across the grain for tenderness.
  • Stir-fry at high heat to seal in flavor.
  • Use your wok or a large skillet for even cooking.

quick substitutions:

extra thoughts:

order of operations:

  • Marinate beef quickly with soy sauce and seasonings.
  • Stir-fry beef until browned, remove from pan.
  • Stir-fry vegetables, then return beef to combine.
  • Serve hot.
