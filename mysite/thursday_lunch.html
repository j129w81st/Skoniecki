Pasta Salad with Focaccia

prep tips:

  • Cook pasta until al dente and rinse under cold water.
  • Chop vegetables (peppers, onions, cucumbers) for added crunch.
  • Toss with a light vinaigrette and chill.

order of operations:

  • Boil and drain pasta.
  • Chop and prepare veggies.
  • Combine pasta, veggies, and dressing.
  • Chill before serving with focaccia.
