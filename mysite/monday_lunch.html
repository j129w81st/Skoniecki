Quinoa Salad with Grilled Chicken, Roasted Peppers, and Onions

prep tips:

  • Rinse quinoa thoroughly before cooking to remove bitterness.
  • Slice peppers and onions into uniform strips for even roasting.
  • Season chicken with salt, pepper, and herbs before grilling.

get ahead:

  • Cook extra quinoa for Tuesday’s lentil soup garnish.
  • Grill extra chicken for Thursday’s pasta salad.

quick substitutions:

  • Replace quinoa with farro or couscous.
  • Use tofu or chickpeas instead of chicken for a vegetarian version.

extra thoughts:

  • Quinoa is high in protein and fiber, making it a hearty base.
  • Leftover roasted peppers add color to wraps or sandwiches.

order of operations:

  • Rinse and cook quinoa according to package instructions.
  • Roast peppers and onions in the oven at 400°F until softened.
  • Grill chicken on medium-high heat until fully cooked.
  • Combine cooked quinoa, roasted veggies, and sliced chicken.
