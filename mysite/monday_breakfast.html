Greek Yogurt with Honey, Strawberries, and Shaved Almonds

prep tips:

  • Use plain Greek yogurt for the best protein-to-flavor balance.
  • Wash, hull, and slice strawberries in advance for convenience.
  • Toast shaved almonds lightly using your stovetop pan or Air Fryer basket.

get ahead:

  • While slicing strawberries, prepare extra for Wednesdayâ€™s lunch topping.

quick substitutions:

  • If out of strawberries, raspberries or blueberries are great alternatives.
  • Swap almonds with walnuts or granola if preferred.

extra thoughts:

  • Greek yogurt offers probiotics and protein, perfect for a quick morning boost.

order of operations:

  • Wash and hull strawberries.
  • Slice strawberries and set aside.
  • Toast almonds carefully to avoid burning.
  • Layer yogurt, honey, strawberries, and almonds.
